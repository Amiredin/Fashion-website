About Afro Style Kenya

Celebrating African Heritage Through Contemporary Fashion

Our Story

Founded in 2018 in the heart of Nairobi, Afro Style Kenya emerged from a passion to celebrate African heritage through contemporary fashion. We believe that clothing is more than just fabric ‚Äì it's a statement of identity, culture, and pride.

Our founder, Grace Wanjiku, started this journey after recognizing the need for high-quality, culturally-inspired fashion that resonates with modern Kenyan families. What began as a small workshop in Westlands has grown into a celebrated fashion house serving customers across East Africa.

Our Mission

To create exceptional fashion that honors African traditions while embracing contemporary design, providing our customers with clothing that makes them feel confident, beautiful, and connected to their heritage.

Our Values

Quality Craftsmanship

Every piece is carefully crafted using premium materials and traditional techniques passed down through generations.

Cultural Pride

We celebrate African heritage through our designs, incorporating traditional patterns and contemporary aesthetics.

Sustainability

We're committed to ethical production practices and supporting local artisans and communities.

Customer Focus

Our customers are at the heart of everything we do, and we strive to exceed their expectations.
